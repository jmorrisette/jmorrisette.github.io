Weekly Recap - 1/13/2020

A pathetic showing from the Vikings sends the 49ers on to the NFC championship game. To make matters worse, the Packers defeat the Seahawks with the help of two scores from Aaron Jones. Derrick Henry goes beast-mode once again as the Titans stun the Ravens. 3 turnovers from Lamar Jackson, ouch! The Texans blow a 24-0 lead and get crushed as Mahomes tosses 5 TDs, 3 of which go to Kelce.
